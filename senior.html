Kalanchoe Senior Center

View Prototype

Challenge Statement:
Create a professional informational website (homepage only) for a senior center meant for retired individuals.

Solution Statement:
A common struggle that retired people deal with is loneliness after at home without kids, as well as increasing health conditions. The purpose of the website is to promote a sense of community and healthier living among adults 65+ years of age that are living alone through group activities, programs, and events.

I wanted to make sure the homepage had all the critical information seniors or loved ones may need without having to search through the other pages (upcoming events, resources offered, etc). This created better accessibility, especially for older individuals who may not be familiar with technology. I wanted the overall feel to be professional, but also welcoming and easy to navigate. Accessibility was my number one priority. I made sure components like buttons and text were larger than normal. I used high contrasting colors and a readable font.
