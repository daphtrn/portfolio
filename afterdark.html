Davis After Dark

View Prototype

Challenge Statement:
Create an app that centralizes information about nightlife events for Davis residents/visitors.

Solution Statement:
Many Davis nightlife goers think it’s difficult to find out about events going on. Davis nightlife goers also have a wide variety of preferences, which fall mainly under either smaller gatherings or larger events. The purpose of this app is to cater to all “types” of preferences, and to also better promote events to those interested.

We wanted to keep personalization a priority, and make sure the users have as much control over their experience as possible. To address that, we added many different filter systems into the app. Users can also add tags to their profile to further personalize their event results. As for the visual design, we made a dark theme with pops of bright color for a “nightlife” feel.
