GiveMeGrub

View Prototype

Challenge Statement:
Create an app for college students that centralizes information related to food accessibility services.

Solution Statement:
College students are usually quite busy and have schedules that don’t always lend itself to being available during usual business hours. This may cause problems with accessing food pantries, or similar organizations if they’re open during usual working hours. College students’ goals are to study and become career ready, and may not always have time to work for money to buy necessities. This also may also cause these students to not have the time or money to buy/make healthy foods.

A few features I plan to include are; a place to receive updates on favorite locations and things that are going on in the community (user posts), a search feature to find specifically what you are looking for, and an explore page for users looking for something new. I wanted the app to look quite friendly, so I used a fun color palette along with a cute egg character. I also wanted it to be easy to navigate for college students in specific, so I made a lot of features similar to that of other social media.
