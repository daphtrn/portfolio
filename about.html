Hi! I’m Daphne and I’m currently a 3rd year studying Design and Cognitive Science at UC Davis.

I’ve always had an eye for visuals, as well as an interest in understanding people. UX design seemed almost like a “too good to be true” intersection between my two biggest passions. I’ve fallen in love with the process of using empathy to create products for people, as well as the satisfaction that comes with solving a problem at hand.
